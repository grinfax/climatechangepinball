About the game

CLimate Change Pinball extends the traditional pinball game into an exciting XR experience that pushes the limit of what a pinball game should be. To win, you must surmount not only the consequences of environmental negligence, but also reap the benefits of caring for the environment and listening to the young voices in our society. Promoting global collaboration to resist the effects of climate change is critical to win this battle, so remember, never drop the ball on climate change!
